Dr. Norman Borlaug

The man who saved a billion lives

Dr Norman Borlaug, third from the left, trains biologists in mexico on how to increase wheat yields- part
of his life long war on hunger

Here's a time line of Dr. Borlaug's life:

  • 1914 - Born in Cresco, Iowa
  • 1933 - Leave his family,s farm to attend the University of Minnesota,
    thanks to a depression era program known as the 'National Youth Admimistration
  • 1935 -Has to stop school and save up more money. Works in the Civilian
    Conservation corps, helping starving Americans. "I" saw her food changed.
